## Invincible Season 5 Comic Connection Explained

Understanding the relationship between the Invincible show and the Invincible comics helps explain why fans search for terms like "Invincible season 5 comic" so frequently.

The show is based on the comic series created by Robert Kirkman, Cory Walker, and Ryan Ottley. Published by Image Comics, the series ran from 2003 to 2018 across 144 issues. It tells the complete story of Mark Grayson from his teenage years discovering his powers through to a full and satisfying conclusion.

The animated adaptation has been following the broad strokes of the comic storyline while making changes to fit the format and to keep even comic readers surprised. This means that while the comics give a general roadmap for where the show is going, the specifics may differ.
