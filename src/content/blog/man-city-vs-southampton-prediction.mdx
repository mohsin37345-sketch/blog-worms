For Manchester City, the motivation is simple: a relentless desire to reach yet another final and cement their legacy as serial winners under Pep Guardiola. ## Manchester City Preview
Manchester City arrive at Wembley in imperious recent form. Their FA Cup route has been highly impressive, punctuated by a commanding quarter-final victory over Liverpool that showcased their elite mentality. 

City’s defining traits remain their suffocating strengths in possession, devastating attacking quality, and absolute midfield control. Their defensive structure allows them to completely dictate the tempo of matches, starving opponents of the ball. Furthermore, their vast big-game experience means they rarely succumb to the pressure of a Wembley occasion. 

Pep Guardiola’s likely approach will be to dominate the ball from the first whistle, pinning Southampton deep into their own half. While Guardiola may rotate slightly given fixture congestion, his starting eleven will still be world-class. The key risks for City are complacency, vulnerability in transition defending if they commit too many bodies forward, and the physical toll of their demanding schedule.

## Southampton Preview
Southampton head into this FA Cup semi-final riding a wave of confidence. Their cup run so far has been nothing short of spectacular, highlighted by a major upset where they successfully eliminated Arsenal. Beating a top-tier side matters immensely psychologically; it proves to the players that they have the blueprint to dismantle elite possession teams.

Embracing the underdog mentality, Southampton will rely heavily on defensive discipline and maintaining a compact shape. Their primary weapons will be a blistering counter-attacking threat and set-piece danger, capitalizing on the few opportunities City will afford them. However, their key weaknesses against elite possession sides lie in concentration lapses during long spells without the ball. To have a chance, Southampton must suffer without the ball gracefully, stay organized, and be ruthlessly clinical when they do break forward.

## Tactical Analysis
The tactical narrative of this Manchester City vs Southampton prediction is a classic attack-versus-defense scenario. 
*   **How will Manchester City dominate possession?** Through Rodri’s orchestration and their inverted full-backs creating midfield overloads.
*   **Can Southampton survive long defensive spells?** Yes, but it requires flawless communication across a back five.
*   **Will Southampton press high or sit deep?** Expect them to sit in a low/mid-block, only triggering a press in specific wide areas to avoid being played through.
*   **How important are wide areas?** Crucial. City will use wingers to stretch Southampton's block, forcing gaps internally.
*   **Can Southampton exploit transitions?** This is their main avenue for success. Quick, direct passes into the channels behind City's high line will be heavily utilized.
*   **What role will set pieces play?** Massive for the underdogs. Southampton must maximize every corner and wide free-kick.
*   **What happens if City score early?** The game could open up dramatically, forcing Southampton to abandon their low block, playing into City's hands.
