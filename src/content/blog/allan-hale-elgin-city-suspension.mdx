## What does suspension mean in football?

In football, suspension usually means a person is temporarily removed from their duties while a process or investigation takes place. It is a neutral act to allow an inquiry to happen. It is not the same as being sacked, and it does imply guilt. The outcome can vary depending on what the investigation finds and what the club decides.
