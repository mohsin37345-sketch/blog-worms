# Morrisons Cafe Closures: Menu Changes and What Shoppers Must Know

**Featured Snippet:**
Morrisons has not announced a permanent nationwide closure of all its in store cafes. However, many locations are currently undergoing significant restructuring, resulting in reduced opening hours, streamlined menu changes, and occasional temporary closures to cut operational costs.

For many shoppers, stopping at the supermarket cafe for a hot drink or a cooked breakfast is a cherished part of the weekly routine. Over the past few months, a wave of concern has swept across social media as customers notice changes at their local branches. Searches regarding Morrisons cafe closures have surged, leaving loyal patrons wondering if their favorite in store dining spots are disappearing for good.

The retail hospitality sector is incredibly challenging right now. Supermarkets are looking very closely at every aspect of their business to ensure profitability, and the cafes are no exception. Understanding what is actually happening behind the scenes helps make sense of the sudden operational shifts.

This guide clarifies exactly what is happening with the Morrisons cafes right now. We will explore the reasons behind the reduced hours, explain the recent menu overhauls, and separate the rumors of total closures from the reality of strategic restructuring.

## What is happening with Morrisons cafes

Here is exactly where things stand across the estate. The supermarket is currently executing a strategic review of its in store dining facilities. While the brand is famous for its affordable cooked meals and friendly service, the cost of running massive commercial kitchens inside supermarkets has skyrocketed. 

As a result, shoppers are noticing a distinct shift in how the cafes operate. Rather than a blanket closure of every single cafe, the company is taking a localized approach. Stores with high dining footfall remain largely unaffected, while quieter locations are seeing immediate cutbacks to their services to prevent financial losses.

## Are Morrisons cafes closing down permanently

This is the key detail you need. There is no official plan to completely eradicate the cafe concept from all Morrisons stores. The brand understands that the cafe is a major draw for specific demographics, particularly older shoppers and families looking for affordable meals during their shopping trips.

However, permanent closures at specific, highly unprofitable locations cannot be entirely ruled out as the company continues to manage its substantial corporate debt. Most of the closures customers are currently experiencing are temporary measures designed to handle staffing shortages or allow for kitchen restructuring rather than permanent shutdowns.

## Why the Morrisons cafe menu has changed

This is an incredibly common observation from regular diners. You may have noticed that the famous all day breakfasts or specific hot meals have been altered or removed entirely. The Morrisons cafe menu has been deliberately streamlined. 

By offering a smaller, more focused menu, the kitchen staff can operate much more efficiently. A reduced menu significantly cuts down on food waste and lowers the cost of ordering diverse ingredients. It also allows the cafes to operate with fewer staff members during quieter periods, which is a crucial component of their current cost saving strategy.

## Changes to Morrisons cafe opening hours

This is perhaps the most noticeable impact for the everyday shopper. Historically, many supermarket cafes stayed open well into the evening to serve after school crowds and late shoppers. That is no longer the case across much of the estate.

Many locations have drastically reduced their opening hours, choosing to close their kitchens by mid afternoon. Management data clearly showed that running a full commercial kitchen late into the evening simply was not generating enough revenue to cover the associated wages and energy costs. If you plan to eat there, checking the revised local hours is now absolutely essential.

## How the broader store closures impact the cafes

It is important to separate the cafe restructuring from the wider news about store shutdowns. The recently announced closure of 17 convenience stores involves the smaller Morrisons Daily format, which do not house cafes anyway. 

The cafe changes are happening inside the massive, traditional supermarket locations. While the two issues are geographically separate, they both stem from the exact same corporate directive: the new private equity owners are aggressively trimming financial fat across every single division of the company.

## Conclusion

The hospitality side of supermarket retail is facing unprecedented financial pressure, and Morrisons is actively adjusting its approach to survive. While the rumors of complete Morrisons cafe closures are largely overstated, the reality is that the dining experience is fundamentally changing. 

Through reduced opening hours, a highly streamlined menu, and occasional temporary closures, the company is fighting to keep the beloved cafe concept financially viable. For shoppers who rely on this service, the best approach is to double check the operating times of your specific local branch before visiting. The affordable supermarket breakfast is not disappearing entirely, but the way it is delivered has changed for good.
