# Barclays Tesco Fuel Cashback: How the 5% Offer Works and Who Qualifies

**Quick answer:** Barclays is offering eligible personal debit card customers 5% cashback on fuel at Tesco petrol stations from 1 May to 31 July 2026. The qualifying spend is between £5 and £200, and cashback is capped at £10 per calendar month per customer. Esso stations linked to Tesco stores are excluded. You need to be registered for Barclays Cashback Rewards through the Barclays app.

Fuel costs remain one of the biggest regular expenses for UK drivers. If you bank with Barclays and fill up at Tesco, a cashback offer running this summer could take a small but useful chunk off your petrol or diesel bill. Here is exactly how it works, who can use it, and what to watch out for.

## What is the Barclays Tesco fuel cashback offer?

Barclays and Tesco have partnered on a fuel cashback offer for summer 2026. From 1 May to 31 July, eligible personal Barclays debit card customers can earn 5% cashback when they buy fuel at Tesco petrol stations.

The offer applies to fuel purchases between £5 and £200. Cashback is capped at £10 per calendar month per customer. The date of purchase determines which month the cashback falls under.

Customers must be registered for Barclays Cashback Rewards in the Barclays app. The offer does not apply at Esso filling stations linked to Tesco stores.

## How much cashback can you get?

The offer gives 5% cashback on qualifying Tesco fuel purchases, up to £10 per calendar month.

Here is how the maths works for different fuel spends:

| Fuel spend | 5% cashback | Important note |
|---|---|---|
| £20 | £1.00 | Within cap |
| £40 | £2.00 | Within cap |
| £100 | £5.00 | Within cap |
| £200 | £10.00 | Hits the monthly cap exactly |
| £250 | £10.00 | Cap applies — cashback does not exceed £10 in a calendar month |

Spending over £200 in a month does not unlock more cashback. The £10 monthly cap is a hard limit. If you spend £200 on qualifying fuel in one calendar month, you will have already reached the maximum.

## Who is eligible for Barclays Tesco fuel cashback?

The offer is for eligible personal Barclays debit card customers. According to Barclays, eligible accounts may include:

- Barclays basic current account
- Barclays Bank account
- Premier current account
- Student Additions or Higher Education account
- Wealth current account

Customers must be aged 18 or over, be a UK resident, and be registered for Barclays Cashback Rewards through the Barclays app.

Not every Barclays customer or card type automatically qualifies. Check the Barclays app and the official offer terms to confirm your eligibility before relying on the deal.

## Which Tesco petrol stations are included?

The offer applies at Tesco petrol stations where Barclays terms allow. This means the branded Tesco petrol filling stations you find next to Tesco supermarkets.

Esso filling stations linked to Tesco stores are excluded from this offer. This is an important distinction because some Tesco stores have nearby Esso sites rather than their own branded petrol station. If you fill up at an Esso site, the Barclays cashback will not apply even if a Tesco Express is on the same forecourt.

Before paying, check that you are at a Tesco-branded petrol station, not an Esso site.

## How do I claim Barclays cashback?

Follow these steps:

1. Open the Barclays app and check that the Tesco fuel cashback offer is visible to you.
2. Make sure you are registered for Barclays Cashback Rewards. If you have not registered, the app should guide you through setup.
3. Read the full offer terms before filling up.
4. Use your eligible personal Barclays debit card to pay for fuel at a participating Tesco petrol station.
5. Keep your receipt until the cashback appears in your account.
6. Check your statement or cashback rewards section in the app. UK media reports suggest cashback may appear as "Barclays Fuel CB" on statements.
7. If qualifying cashback has not appeared after the stated processing time, contact Barclays.

## When does the offer start and end?

The offer runs from 1 May 2026 to 31 July 2026. That gives eligible customers three calendar months to earn cashback.

Barclays has not confirmed an extension beyond 31 July 2026. If the offer changes, is extended, or ends early, Barclays should update the terms in the app.

Always check the latest terms before filling up, especially towards the end of July.

## Does Tesco Esso fuel count?

No. Barclays excludes Esso stations linked to Tesco stores from this fuel cashback offer.

This matters because some drivers associate Tesco with Esso-branded forecourts, particularly where an Esso site has a Tesco Express shop attached. For this offer, only Tesco-branded petrol filling stations qualify. If you are not sure whether your local site is Tesco-branded or Esso, check the signage on the forecourt canopy before you pay.

## Is this the same as Tesco Clubcard points?

No. These are two separate things.

**Barclays cashback** is money back from Barclays, paid into your bank account on qualifying fuel purchases made with an eligible Barclays debit card. It is a Barclays reward, not a Tesco reward.

**Tesco Clubcard points** are part of Tesco's own loyalty scheme. Tesco Bank says Clubcard Credit Card holders can collect Clubcard points on fuel at Tesco petrol stations (excluding Esso). Clubcard vouchers can also be used on fuel at Tesco petrol stations and at Esso sites with a Tesco Express.

Whether both the Barclays cashback and Clubcard points can apply to the same purchase depends on the terms of each scheme. Do not assume they stack unless both sets of terms confirm it.

## Can you get cashback at Tesco petrol station?

The word "cashback" can mean two different things at a petrol station, so it is worth being clear.

**Reward cashback:** If you are asking about the Barclays offer, then yes — eligible Barclays debit card customers can earn 5% cashback on qualifying fuel at Tesco petrol stations during the offer period.

**Cash withdrawal cashback:** If you mean taking cash out when paying at the till, that is a completely separate question. Cash withdrawal cashback depends on Tesco's payment facilities, store policy, and your card type. It has nothing to do with the Barclays fuel reward.

This article is about the Barclays reward cashback offer, not cash withdrawals.

## Can I get cashback on fuel?

Yes, some offers do give cashback on fuel. The Barclays Tesco fuel cashback offer is one current example for eligible customers.

Fuel cashback offers usually come with conditions: specific cards, specific stations, spending limits, monthly caps, and fixed dates. The Barclays offer is no different. It only applies to eligible personal Barclays debit card customers buying fuel at Tesco petrol stations from 1 May to 31 July 2026, with a £10 monthly cap.

Do not buy fuel you do not need just to chase cashback. The saving only makes sense on fuel you would buy anyway.

## Can I get cashback from Tesco?

Tesco customers can get value through several routes: Clubcard points on shopping and fuel, Clubcard vouchers and partner offers, and the Tesco Bank Clubcard Credit Card for enhanced rewards.

The Barclays Tesco fuel cashback, however, is a Barclays offer run in partnership with Tesco. It is not a general Tesco cashback scheme open to all shoppers. You need an eligible Barclays debit card and you must be registered for Barclays Cashback Rewards.

If you do not bank with Barclays, this specific offer does not apply to you. Tesco Clubcard rewards remain available separately through Tesco's own scheme.

**Reality:** Only eligible personal Barclays debit card customers registered for Barclays Cashback Rewards can earn the fuel cashback. Not all accounts qualify automatically.

**Mistake:** "Esso stations linked to Tesco are included."
**Reality:** Barclays specifically excludes Esso filling stations from this offer. Only Tesco-branded petrol stations qualify.

**Reality:** Cashback is capped at £10 per calendar month per customer. Spending more than £200 on qualifying fuel in a month will not increase your cashback beyond £10.

**Mistake:** "Clubcard points and Barclays cashback are the same thing."
**Reality:** These are two entirely separate reward schemes. Barclays cashback comes from Barclays. Clubcard points come from Tesco. They are different products run by different companies.

**Reality:** You must use an eligible personal Barclays debit card. Other bank cards, credit cards, or even non-qualifying Barclays cards may not count.
