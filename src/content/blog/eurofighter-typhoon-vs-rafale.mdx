The debate of **Eurofighter Typhoon vs Rafale** comes down to distinct design philosophies: pure high-altitude interception versus omnirole flexibility.

| Feature | Eurofighter Typhoon | Dassault Rafale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Strength** | High-altitude speed and climb | Strike flexibility & naval capability |
| **Origin** | UK, Germany, Italy, Spain | France |
| **Engines** | 2 × Eurojet EJ200 | 2 × Snecma M88 |
| **Carrier Capable**| No | Yes (Rafale M) |
