A frequent question is regarding the **Eurofighter Typhoon generation**. It is widely classified as a 4th-generation or 4.5-generation fighter, distinct from 5th-generation stealth fighters.

| Feature | Eurofighter Typhoon (4.5 Gen) | Typical 5th Gen (e.g. F-35) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stealth Design** | Conventional (Non-stealth) | Very Low Observable |
| **Weapons Payload** | Heavy, external carriage | Internal weapons bays |
| **Sensor Fusion** | High (Captor-E AESA Radar) | Fully integrated |
| **Speed & Agility**| Extremely High | Moderate / Optimized |
