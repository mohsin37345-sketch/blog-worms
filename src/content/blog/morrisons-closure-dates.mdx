# Morrisons Closure Dates 2026: Full Timeline for Affected Stores

**Featured Snippet:**
The confirmed Morrisons closure dates span across a two month window. The initial wave of the 17 affected convenience stores shut their doors permanently in April, with the final remaining locations scheduled to cease all trading by the end of May. 

Whenever a major retailer announces a restructuring program, the immediate question from the public is incredibly simple: when exactly is it happening? Understanding the precise timeline is crucial for shoppers who need to alter their daily routines and for staff members trying to plan their financial futures. Recently, search volumes for Morrisons closure dates have spiked dramatically as the reality of the high street shutdowns sets in.

Closing a retail business is a complex logistical operation. You cannot simply lock the doors overnight. Stock must be depleted, equipment must be dismantled, and leases must be formally surrendered. Because of these complexities, the supermarket chain has opted for a staggered approach.

This guide breaks down the complete timeline of events. We will explain exactly when the shutters are coming down on these 17 locations, why the company chose a phased approach, and what shoppers should expect to see happening inside the stores during their final trading days.

## The full timeline for Morrisons closures

Here is the exact schedule dictating the end of these retail locations. The company enacted a heavily structured, two month phased closure program. The timeline officially commenced in April, marking the beginning of the end for the affected Morrisons Daily branches.

The initial wave saw 16 stores cease operations rapidly during the first phase of the rollout. Following this rapid reduction, the company moved to finalize the remaining logistics. The final store in this specific batch of 17 is confirmed to permanently lock its doors before the conclusion of May, bringing this particular chapter of corporate restructuring to a definitive close.

## Why the closures are staggered across two months

This is why the dates are spread out rather than happening on a single day. Shutting down commercial retail space requires immense coordination. By staggering the Morrisons closure dates, the logistics teams can move methodically from region to region without overwhelming their resources.

This phased approach allows store managers to safely manage the rundown of perishable stock, heavily discounting items to minimize food waste before the final day. Furthermore, it provides the human resources department with a vital window of time to conduct individual consultations with staff members, exploring redeployment options at nearby larger supermarkets before resorting to compulsory redundancy.

## How to find your specific local closure date

Because the dates vary slightly from town to town, finding your exact local timeline requires a bit of attention. The overarching corporate timeline dictates an April and May window, but the specific final trading day for an individual high street branch depends entirely on localized lease agreements and stock levels.

If your local store is on the closure list, the most accurate source of information is the store itself. Management is required to display prominent physical notices on the entrance doors and at the checkout counters detailing the exact final day of service. Relying on these physical signs is far safer than trusting neighborhood rumors.

## What happens after the final dates pass

Once the May deadline concludes and all 17 stores are officially closed, the focus shifts entirely to the real estate. The physical properties will be stripped of all corporate branding, refrigeration units, and point of sale systems. 

These empty units will then be handed back to their respective commercial landlords. For the local high street, this means the introduction of vacant retail spaces. Shoppers will need to transition fully to alternative convenience stores or begin utilizing the larger out of town Morrisons supermarkets for their daily needs.

## Will there be more closure dates announced

This is the lingering fear for many retail analysts and loyal customers. As of right now, Morrisons has only confirmed the dates for this specific batch of 17 convenience stores. However, the corporate entity remains under immense pressure to service the heavy debt acquired during its 2021 private equity buyout.

While no further dates have been officially published, corporate reviews of store performance are a continuous, ongoing process. If other locations begin to show sustained unprofitability due to rising rent or energy costs, it is entirely possible that a new timeline of closures could be announced later in the financial year.
